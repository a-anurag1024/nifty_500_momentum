## Supporting Research & References

Below are collected academic and industry papers that support the motivation behind combining
momentum signals with news sentiment:

### 1. **Sentiment-Based Momentum Strategy**
*Kim et al., 2018 — Journal of International Financial Markets, Institutions & Money*  
Shows that momentum strategies enhanced with sentiment significantly outperform traditional price-only momentum.  
Link: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S1057521918300413

### 2. **Combining News Sentiment and Technical Analysis to Predict Trend Reversal**
*Cagliero et al., 2019*  
Demonstrates that combining technical indicators (momentum) with news-sentiment features improves predictive accuracy for market trend reversals.  
Link: https://www.sentic.net/sentire2019cagliero.pdf

### 3. **News Sentiment and Momentum**
*Huynh & Smith, 2013 — Auckland Centre for Financial Research*  
Finds that stock momentum is partially driven by how markets react to news, and that “news-momentum” portfolios generate strong returns.  
Link: https://acfr.aut.ac.nz/__data/assets/pdf_file/0006/29967/397984.pdf

### 4. **Technical Patterns and News Sentiment in Stock Markets**
*Leippold et al., 2024 — Finance Research Letters*  
Shows that technical pattern signals become more effective when combined with news sentiment; particularly helpful in high-momentum and small-cap contexts.  
Link: https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/pii/S2405918824000308

### 5. **Parametric portfolio policy with momentum-based sentiment trading strategy**
*Lee et al., 2025 — PLOS ONE*  
Proposes a sentiment-augmented portfolio construction method that outperforms classical momentum and mean-variance approaches, especially during volatile periods.  
Link: https://journals.plos.org/plosone/article?id=10.1371/journal.pone.0335462
